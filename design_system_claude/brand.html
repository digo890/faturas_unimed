Brand

Unimed's visual identity is anchored by the official brand symbol and the institutional navy. Sub-brands (Saúde, Odonto, Seguros) reuse the mark with distinct wordmarks; the ANS seal is a regulatory attribution, never decorative.

Primary Mark

The symbol alone is used in app icons, avatars, and the nav header. Minimum size is 24 × 24 px. Always use the official SVG from assets/unimed-symbol.svg.

Regulatory & Partner Marks

ANS (Agência Nacional de Saúde Suplementar) attribution is required in the footer of any Unimed Saúde or Odonto surface. The ANS seal and registration number are a legal obligation — never remove, recolor, or resize below 24 px high.

Clear space & sizing

Reserve a margin equal to the height of the symbol's base flame around every lockup. Never crowd the mark against text, edges, or imagery.

Usage rules

✓

Do place the mark on white, Primary Blue, or Unimed Green. Always use an approved SVG from assets/.

✓

Do keep the minimum clear space equal to the symbol's base flame on all sides.

✗

Don't recolor the mark outside the palette, add drop shadows, or apply gradients.

✗

Don't rotate, stretch, or outline the wordmark. Place marks on low-contrast photography without a backing plate.
